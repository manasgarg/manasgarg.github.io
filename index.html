The Many Mes

Inside me, there are many mes. One me works at Adobe as an Director of Machine Learning, solves technical problems and manages projects. Another me is curious about the physical, social and psychological environments, tries to understand them and asks the eternal question "What's the meaning of life, universe and everything?". Yet another me loves to travel with camera in hand, spends time in jungles and mountains and hugs very old trees. And a significant part of me is a son, a husband and a father.

I am the sum total of these mes; and a few more. I go by the name of Manas.

On and off, I have written on multiple subjects. Archives page has the list of everything I have written and this page has some of my favorites.
