Standing on a cliff,  
 Near the seashore,  
 Facing the wind,  
 With arms stretched,  
 And water droplets falling on my face,  
 My eyes closed,  
 Facing the sky,  
 And a wish comes out,  
 May I vanish now,  
 And live on forever...
